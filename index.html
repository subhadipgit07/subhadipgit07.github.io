More Than a Website.
A Blueprint for India's Football Future.

Tigers Global Blueprint aims to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by providing every Indian footballer—regardless of financial background or location—with access to elite football education, scientific training, nutrition, analytics, and scouting.

OUR VISION

Creating India's Football Identity

Instead of copying European football, we believe India should build its own football identity based on its climate, culture, nutrition, playing conditions, grassroots system and player development pathway.

Our long-term vision is to create a complete football ecosystem where every child, parent, coach, academy, and scout contributes to building a stronger Indian football future.

POSITION GUIDE

Train Like a Professional

Every position has unique responsibilities. Learn the technical, tactical, physical and mental skills required to master your role.

Goalkeeper

Reflexes, positioning, distribution, communication.

Centre Back

Defending, aerial duels, leadership, passing.

Full Back

Speed, recovery runs, crossing and defending.

Midfielder

Scanning, vision, passing and game control.

Winger

Acceleration, dribbling, creativity and crossing.

Striker

Finishing, movement, composure and pressing.
